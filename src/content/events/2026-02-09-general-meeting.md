On the second Monday of even-numbered months, we hold a general meeting that is open to the public. All are welcome and encouraged to attend.

## What to Expect

General meetings typically include:
- Updates from the board on ongoing projects
- Presentations from guest speakers on topics of neighborhood interest
- Open forum for neighbor questions and concerns
- Voting on any pending neighborhood matters

## Location

Sylvan Learning Center is located at 4411 NE Fremont St, in the Beaumont Village shopping area. Street parking is available.

## Can't Attend in Person?

Contact us if you'd like information about remote attendance options.
