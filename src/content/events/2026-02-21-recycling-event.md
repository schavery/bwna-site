Bring your clean styrofoam and old electronics for responsible recycling at this popular neighborhood event!

## Accepted Items

### Styrofoam
- Block styrofoam (packing material)
- Styrofoam peanuts
- Clean food containers (rinsed)

**Not accepted:** Styrofoam with tape, labels, or food residue

### Electronics
- Computers, laptops, tablets
- Monitors and TVs
- Printers and scanners
- Cell phones
- Cables and chargers
- Small appliances

**Not accepted:** Large appliances, batteries (take to Metro transfer stations)

## Details

- **Cost:** Free for most items; small fee for monitors/TVs
- **Limit:** Reasonable household quantities
- **Location:** Church parking lot - follow signs
